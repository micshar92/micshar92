What does "Yumejo" mean?

In Japanese, "yume (夢)" means "dream" and "-jo (女)" is a suffix that means "woman" or "girl." So "Yumejo" means i dunno i made it up "dream girl." It's the name of an app in my novel by the same name.

One of the characters is princess Jacquelyn Lux Estelle Violette-Worthingham, or Jakki. Her fictional address is:

Jacquelyn Lux Estelle Violette-Worthingham, 1 Violette Lane, Thystia, Auros

Her birthday is, I dunno, on the 10th of December. I just now made that up.
